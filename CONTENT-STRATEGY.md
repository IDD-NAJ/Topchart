## SEO Best Practices for Content

### On-Page SEO
- **Title Tags:** Include primary keyword, brand name, under 60 characters
- **Meta Descriptions:** Include keywords, compelling CTA, under 160 characters
- **H1 Tags:** One per page, includes primary keyword
- **H2/H3 Tags:** Include secondary keywords, semantic structure
- **URL Structure:** Short, keyword-rich, hyphen-separated
- **Internal Links:** Link to related content and service pages
- **External Links:** Link to authoritative sources where relevant

### Content Structure
- **Introduction:** Hook reader, state problem, preview solution
- **Body:** Comprehensive information, step-by-step guides
- **FAQ Section:** Address common questions, schema markup
- **Conclusion:** Summary, CTA to relevant service page
- **Word Count:** 1500-2500 words for pillar pages, 800-1500 for supporting content

### Schema Markup
- **Article Schema:** For all blog posts
- **FAQ Schema:** For FAQ sections
- **HowTo Schema:** For step-by-step guides
- **Breadcrumb Schema:** For navigation
- **Organization Schema:** Author attribution
